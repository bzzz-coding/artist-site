Paper Making

Paper making was one of the most "unusual" studio art courses I took in college and I absolutelly loved it! I collected and experiemented with all sorts of plant parts (including corn husks and pineapple leaves!) to extract the fiber, filter and press into paper of various colors and textures. The uniqueness of each sheet then inspired me to make paper sculptures and an artist book.

Sculpture and Installation Work

Sculptures do not have to be strong and heavy. Paper redefines weight and strength.
